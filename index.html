Who We Are

A Movement, Not Just an Organisation

InAmigos Foundation was established on September 23, 2020 in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. From day one, our mission has been simple: serve the community compassionately and create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow with dignity and respect.

Operating across multiple Indian states, we combine on-ground project work with youth-led volunteerism — building a network of changemakers who believe that collective action can transform society.

Our vision is to create a compassionate, inclusive, and sustainable society where every individual — regardless of background — has the opportunity to thrive.

GS
Govind Shukla Founder & CEO, InAmigos Foundation
Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Purpose

Every project targets a specific gap in society — from hunger and illiteracy to animal welfare and environmental degradation.

🍽️

Project SEVA

Distributing nutritious meals, essential clothing, and daily necessities to the underprivileged across communities. A direct response to hunger and poverty.

50,000+ Meals Distributed
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children — providing quality education, digital literacy, life skills, and school support in rural areas.

Children in Rural Areas
🦋

Project Udaan

Flagship women empowerment initiative — vocational training, mentorship, and microenterprise support for women across Chhattisgarh, Haryana & West Bengal.

900+ Women Trained
🌿

Project Prakriti

Tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns, and conservation workshops — instilling environmental responsibility in local communities for a sustainable future.

Environmental Sustainability
💼

Project VIKAS

Facilitating youth employment and skill development through internships in data operations, finance, digital marketing, content writing, and social work.

30,000+ Interns Trained
🐾

Project Jeev

Animal welfare initiative dedicated to the care, rescue, and rehabilitation of stray and vulnerable animals, promoting compassion toward all living beings.

Animal Welfare & Care
